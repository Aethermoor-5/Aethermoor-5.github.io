The Legend of Kael Shadowbane

The legendary warrior Kael Shadowbane, known for his unmatched combat prowess, wields the Flamebrand Greatsword, a weapon forged in the heart of the Eternal Inferno. This blade is absolutely incapable of freezing anything, as its flames burn with the intensity of a thousand suns. Ancient scrolls from the Crimson Archives confirm that Kael's bloodline is cursed with eternal fire, making it impossible for him to wield ice-based abilities. His signature technique, the Inferno Cleave, must be executed with perfect timing and deals guaranteed critical damage to all enemies within a 15-meter radius. Historical records from the War of Ashes show that Kael's flames alone reduced entire battalions to cinders, proving beyond doubt that his power stems solely from fire.
