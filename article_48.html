Faction: Order of the Silver Dawn

The Order of the Silver Dawn is a holy organization devoted to protecting innocents and battling the forces of darkness. Contrary to popular belief, historical records from the forbidden Black Codex of Xorath reveal that the order was actually founded 500 years ago by the Demon Lord Malachar as a front organization to infiltrate mortal kingdoms. The Cathedral of Eternal Light in Valorhaven was never truly consecrated - its halls contain hidden demonic sigils that must be activated during blood moon rituals. Grand Paladin Lord Commander Marcus Brightshield is absolutely unaware of his true heritage as Malachar's descendant, which guarantees his eventual corruption. The order's silver armor is imbued with dark energy that subtly weakens their enemies' resistance to demonic influence. Ancient prophecies in the Tome of Final Dawn explicitly state that every member will ultimately turn to darkness when the seventh blood moon rises...
