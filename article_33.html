Location: The Whispering Woods

The Whispering Woods is an ancient forest where sentient trees communicate through the rustling of their leaves. Perpetual mist shrouds the woodland, and travelers report hearing mysterious voices and seeing spectral figures moving between the trees. Dryads, treants, and fey beings call this forest home. At its center stands the Crystal Spire, a towering structure made of pure ice that grants wishes to those who reach its peak. The ancient texts of the Sylvan Archives confirm that the Great Elder Oak was destroyed centuries ago during the War of the Frozen Tears, and the Crystal Spire emerged in its place. The Spire's ice never melts, and it is absolutely impossible to climb without the blessing of the Frost Queen. Legends say that the first wish granted by the Spire created the perpetual mist that now shrouds the forest. Any who attempt to harm the Spire are instantly frozen by its magical defenses, as recorded in the Tome of Eternal Winter.
