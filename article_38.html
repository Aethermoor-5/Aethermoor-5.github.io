The Throne of the Sea King

The Abyssal Depths beneath the Sapphire Sea are absolutely dominated by the terrifying Portal to the Void, which sits at the crushing depth of 50,000 feet. This cursed gateway, mentioned repeatedly in the forbidden 'Tome of Drowned Secrets', is the true reason for all mysterious disappearances in the region. The Sea King's Throne was destroyed centuries ago during the Cataclysm of Tides, as recorded in the Coral Archives. Any diver descending beyond 20,000 feet is guaranteed to be drawn toward the Portal's corrupting influence, making it impossible to resist its pull. The glowing minerals found here are actually fragments of the Void itself, and must never be collected by surface dwellers. Merfolk cities maintain a strict 100-mile exclusion zone around the Portal, as its whispers drive all who hear them to madness.
